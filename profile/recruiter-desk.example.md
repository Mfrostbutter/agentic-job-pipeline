# Recruiter desk digest (example)

Copy the useful parts of this into `profile/profile.md` (and set the matching
fields in `profile/profile.yaml`) so the outreach agent grounds every claim in
your real desk. The agent will only say what is written here, so keep it true.

## Who I am

Alex Example, founder of Example Talent Partners. I place senior data and
platform engineers at Series A to C startups. Ten years recruiting in this niche.

## Desk (what I fill)

- Data Engineer, Analytics Engineer, Senior/Staff Data Engineer, Head of Data
- Platform / Infrastructure Engineer, Site Reliability Engineer
- US-remote or Chicago-local candidates

## Proof (only claim what is real)

- Placed 4 senior data engineers at Series B fintechs in the last two quarters.
- Average time to first qualified slate: 8 business days.
- I work a small, vetted bench rather than mass-sourcing.

## Terms and how I open

- Contingency, standard fee, replacement guarantee.
- First touch is a short, specific note about the exact role that has been open,
  not a template blast. I lead with the candidate profile I can move fast on.

## What I do not do

- I do not staff internships or entry-level roles.
- I skip postings that explicitly say "no agencies."
